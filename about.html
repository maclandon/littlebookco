Meet Elle

A mom who made one book, and
couldn't stop.

Hi! I'm Elle — mom, homeschool educator, and total craft nerd. I started Little Book Co. because I couldn't find a workbook that felt personal enough for my daughter.

She'd flip past worksheets in seconds. But when I made one with her name on every page, her favorite characters, and activities I knew she'd love — she asked to do it every morning.

That was three years ago. Now I make them for families all over the country, and that moment of a child opening their very own personalized book never gets old.

What I Believe

Every child deserves to feel like the main character.

💛

Learning is personal

When a child sees their own name in a book, something clicks. It becomes theirs. That spark is everything.

🌱

Play is the best teacher

Every activity is designed to feel like play, not homework. Kids learn faster when they're having fun.

💚

Quality over quantity

I'd rather make one book a family uses for 3 years than sell 10 they toss after a month. Dry-erase for life.

The Story

From one book to thousands of families.

2021
The first book

Made my daughter her first personalized workbook at the kitchen table. She cried (happy tears) when she saw her name. I cried too.

2022
Etsy launch

Friends started asking. Then their friends. Opened the Etsy shop and hit 100 sales by month three.

2023
TikTok goes viral

A 60-second video of my daughter opening her book hit 798K views. Inbox flooded. Etsy overwhelmed. Best problem I've ever had.

2026
This website!

Launched littlebookco.org so I can serve families directly, build a real community, and keep making books kids love.
